Why Follow This Routine

  • Breaks: Help prevent boredom and mental fatigue. Short breaks ensure you stay focused and productive throughout your study sessions.
  • Nature Time: Refreshes and rejuvenates, enhancing overall well-being. Spending time in nature helps reduce stress and improve mental clarity.
  • Note-taking: Reinforces and helps to consume the concepts effectively. Taking notes aids in understanding and remembering the material more thoroughly.
  • Evening Planning: Prepares for the next day’s study, ensuring continuity and effective use of time. Choosing the next day's chapter and researching important topics keeps you organized and ready for the upcoming tasks.
  • Focus on One Chapter per Day: Helps to learn and understand the material more easily without overlapping with other subjects or chapters. This focused approach prevents cognitive overload and improves retention of information.
  • Routine Flexibility: Feel free to adjust the routine as needed but remember that focusing on one chapter per day is crucial for optimal learning.
